Verbally is a floating dictation app for Android 14+.

Tap the floating bubble beside a text field, speak, confirm the recording, and
Verbally inserts the resulting text at the current cursor.

Highlights:

• Floating dictation bubble for Android text fields
• Bring-your-own-key provider setup
• Speech-to-text through OpenAI, Soniox, Groq, or Deepgram
• Text cleanup through OpenAI or Gemini
• Local history retention controls
• Local dictionary and snippets
• Direct cursor insertion with clipboard fallback only when needed

Verbally uses Android AccessibilityService to detect editable fields, show the
floating dictation bubble, and insert or verify dictated text at the cursor. The app
shows an in-app disclosure and asks for consent before opening Accessibility
settings.

Verbally has no backend, account system, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, crash
reporting SDK, or cloud sync. Audio is temporary and is deleted after success,
failure, or cancellation. AI provider requests are sent only to the providers you
select with your own API keys.
