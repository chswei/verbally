## Why

The refreshed floating bubble palette corrected the overall white-and-blue direction, but it accidentally removed the translucency that helps the overlay feel light and layered above other apps. The user wants that floating transparency restored without undoing the new Verbally-aligned color scheme.

## What Changes

- Restore semi-transparent surfaces for the ready bubble and the secondary active controls.
- Restore a semi-transparent brand-blue fill for the primary confirm/progress control while keeping the same white-and-blue hierarchy.
- Keep the launcher icon and the rest of the prior palette refresh unchanged.

## Capabilities

### Modified Capabilities

- `floating-dictation-overlay`: Keep the refreshed Verbally bubble palette while preserving translucent overlay surfaces.
